Permissions

Some rules about the sketch-a-thon, and the things I'll be able to draw.

  • 1 $10 ticket = 1 10minute sketch. There are add-ons to increase the amount of tickets, but you can also purchase another ticket.
  • If it doesn't reach my own personal standard, I'll extend a piece to at most 5 more minutes.
  • You may purchase tickets on others' behalf.
  • Requests to draw nsfw art will politely denied.
  • Feel free to challenge be with art otherwise ! As long as it's safe to draw on stream it'll be okay. :)

The raffle should be happening at the end of the stream. You'll be contacted through wherever I can find you (probably whatever you used on Ko-fi).
